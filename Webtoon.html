Webtoon is a korean based comic website/application popular among young teens. The app lets you conveniently access your favorite webtoons with one touch. You can also easily become a comic artist by applying to be a webtoon artist. Webtoon also allows patrons to support their favorite artist by donating to them. The content of the comics range from Romance, Thriller, Horror, Slice of Life, Sports, and Fantasy. Webtoon is also a safe environment because inappropriate content is filtered out. Additionally, the app has a comment feature, where artists can get feedback from enthusiastic fans and where fans can rant about their predictions. It is a great app to have on a mobile device, you can whip out your phone during your free time and dive into an intriguing story. Even though Webtooon is originally in Korean, fans have translated their favorite comics into over 50 languages. Webtoon even has its own social media pages, where fans can interact among themselves and be updates on the latest comics. Click here to access the amazing Webtoon website.
